Terms and Conditions

We guarantee the plant's variety and fruiting potential if properly cared for. The health and productivity of the plant depend on the treatment, environmental conditions, and temperature. Each plant is certified and verified by the District Horticulture Office and thoroughly checked for diseases like nematodes before being delivered to customers.

NHB Guidelines and Certification

Our nursery is registered with the National Horticulture Board (NHB) and holds a 2-star rating. We sell only those plants that are examined by horticulture experts to be disease-free. We strictly follow NHB rules, including grafted plant certification, rootstock quality, labeling, packaging, pest and disease control, and nursery hygiene.

Our Commitments

At Om Vishnu Fruit Nursery, we sell plants only after fulfilling all required NHB guidelines and health certification processes. We prioritize our customer's trust and aim to deliver healthy, high-quality plants with verified documentation and inspection.
