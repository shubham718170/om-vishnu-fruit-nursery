NHB Nursery Compliance

We follow all NHB (National Horticulture Board) guidelines for nursery operations. This includes proper record keeping, mother plant maintenance, use of certified rootstocks, pest and disease control, and inspection-ready infrastructure.

We ensure that all our fruit plants are cultivated and sold in accordance with these standards.
